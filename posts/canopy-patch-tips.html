How we patch kite canopies (clean, strong, discreet)

Published 2025-05-18 · by KiteRepair.pt

We match canopy fabric weight and bias direction, then use a stepped patch with reinforced perimeter stitching. This keeps the repair strong while minimizing visual impact and flutter.

Materials

  • Brand-matched ripstop when possible
  • High-strength adhesive film for placement
  • UV-stable thread

Process

  1. Clean and trim edges, align bias.
  2. Apply inner patch, then outer reinforcement.
  3. Secure with straight + zigzag seams, tension checked.
